Countdown Numbers Game Solver

Also known as Summle, Countle, Numbers Game, or Additional

How it works: Enter a target and up to 6 numbers, then click Solve to find all possible solutions.

Solutions are grouped by step count (fewest first) and hidden by default. Click any group to expand it.

⭐ Star icon indicates the group contains a "golden path" solution where each result flows naturally into the next step.
